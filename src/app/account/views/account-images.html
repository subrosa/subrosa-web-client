Photo ID

Photo IDs are similar to passport photos and have the following requirements:

  • Must include your full face, front view, eyes open.
  • Must be a recent picture representing how you actually look for the duration of the game. You may not take a picture with long hair and then shave it, for example.
  • No garments or accessories that obscure your face or hair, including hats, sunglasses, scarves, etc. Prescription eyeglasses are okay.

Action Photo

Action Photos are used to show off your skills. They have the following requirements:

  • Must show you being awesome.
