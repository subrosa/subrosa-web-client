Your Responsibilities as Game Master

  • Recruit Players to Join your Game

    Call your mother, text your friends, email your co-workers; a happy game is one with lots of players.

  • Arbitrate In-Game Disputes

    Disputes may arise if two players disagree on the outcome of game interaction. The Game Master interface includes a dispute management system to facilitate expedient dispute arbitration.

  • Engage your Audience

    Your players will play to the best of their ability if they are engaged. Think of creative ways to get their attention. You could offer special prizes for meeting certain criteria, for example.
